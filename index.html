The Emergency Response Crisis

Current systems are fragmented and slow. Seconds save lives.

Delayed Response

Ambulances and responders often take too long to reach accident scenes due to poor coordination.

Lack of Coordination

Police, hospitals, and emergency services operate in disconnected systems, causing vital data loss.

Information Gap

Bystanders often don’t know who to contact or how to provide accurate location details.

The Solution – ResQ AI

ResQ is a mobile emergency response platform that works like a smart 911 system. We bridge the gap between reporting and responding.

  • Direct connection to Hospitals & Police
  • GPS-precision dispatching
  • Immediate access to Legal Assistance
